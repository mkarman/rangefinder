Built for the UxS Test & Evaluation Community

RangeFinder was created by test professionals who understand the friction of finding suitable ranges for unmanned systems operations. From FAA-coordinated airspace to deep-water UUV test corridors, our affiliate network spans the full spectrum of UxS test environments.

We streamline the connection between test teams and range operators — reducing lead times, improving safety coordination, and expanding access to specialized test infrastructure across the country.
